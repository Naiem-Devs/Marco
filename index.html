What we value.

We live these values every day, in everything we do.

We work to a greater cause

Giving back is in our DNA. We don’t need to choose between doing good and doing well.

We’re customer obsessed

We deliver exceptional experiences to customers and merchants, adding value in everything we do.

We succeed together

We’re a high-performing team, believing shared success to be significantly more important than individual performances.

We’re open and respectful

We listen, learn, support and grow through honest, constructive communication. We both challenge and are open to feedback.

We’re pioneers

We back ourselves and aren’t afraid to fail. We have the courage to challenge the status quo, and an action bias. We do more with less.

We’re accountable to ourselves and each other

With clear and transparent objectives and results, we are accountable for the promises we make to each other
